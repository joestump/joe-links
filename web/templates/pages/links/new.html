    Variable URLs

    Use $varname in the destination URL to create parameterized links.

    Example

    slug: jira
    url: https://jira.example.com/browse/$ticket
    go/jira/FOO-1234 → jira.example.com/browse/FOO-1234

    Each $var in the URL becomes a path segment after the slug. Variables are URL-encoded automatically.

    Visibility

    publicAnyone can follow the link, even without an account.
    privateLink works for anyone with the URL, but won't appear in Browse or profiles.
    secureRequires login. Only owners and explicitly shared users can follow it.
